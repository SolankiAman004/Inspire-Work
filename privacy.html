Privacy Policy — Inspire Works

Effective Date: 20-10-2025

At Inspire Works, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or engage with our freelancing and creative services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal and non-personal information when you interact with our website or agency, including:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, project details, and payment information.
  • Non-Personal Information: Browser type, IP address, device, and usage data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and requests.
  • To deliver project updates and proposals.
  • To improve our website and services.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use modern encryption and security measures to safeguard your information. Your data is never sold or shared without consent.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. You can disable cookies via your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We use trusted tools like Google Analytics and secure payment gateways. These partners are required to protect your data confidentiality.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for legitimate business or legal purposes, after which it is securely deleted.

7. Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data anytime by emailing us at [your email].

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has submitted personal data, please contact us immediately.
